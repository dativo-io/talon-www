Rule of thumb

Use Talon when the review asks for proof.

Talon is the right first step when a customer, DPO, security team, or auditor asks what AI traffic left your environment, which policy allowed it, and whether you can verify the record.

Talon answers:

- What data left?
- Where did it go?
- Was PII handled?
- Was cost capped?
- Were tools governed?
- Can the record be verified?
