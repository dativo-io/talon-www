Why it exists

PII proxies and observability dashboards are not enough.

Talon is built for the operational questions European teams face when AI agents touch customer data, tools, vendors, and regulated workflows.

Govern before execution

Identify callers, scan input, classify data, evaluate policy, govern tools, redact when needed, and only then forward to the LLM provider.

Prove what happened

Every interaction creates signed evidence with policy decision, PII findings, model, cost, hashes, and verification status.

Designed for EMEA

Self-hostable, EU-focused, and built around GDPR Article 30-style evidence, NIS2 incident review, and EU AI Act governance workflows.
