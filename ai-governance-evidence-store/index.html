Why evidence matters

Logs explain. Evidence proves.

For AI governance reviews, incident response, and auditor handoff, teams need structured records that show who called what, which policy was active, what was allowed or denied, what data tier was detected, which model was used, and whether the record was modified later.

🧾

Every decision recorded

Allowed, denied, redacted, filtered, and failed requests all produce reviewable records.

🔐

Tamper-evident by design

Evidence is signed so later changes invalidate verification.

📤

Exportable for review

Export CSV or JSON evidence for compliance, security, FinOps, or customer reviews.

Auditor handoff

Export, sample, verify.

A practical evidence workflow is simple: export records for a time window, sample relevant evidence IDs, verify signatures, and attach the export plus verification note to the audit package.

talon audit export \
  --format csv \
  --from 2026-02-01 \
  --to 2026-02-28
