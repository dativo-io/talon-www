Controls

What Talon adds to AI traffic.

Talon sits at the provider boundary. It checks policy before requests are forwarded and records signed evidence after each decision.

🔐

PII and content controls

Detect, redact, warn, or block sensitive data before it reaches an LLM provider.

🇪🇺

Provider and region control

Apply EU strict, EU preferred, or global routing rules based on your data posture.

🧾

Signed evidence

Record policy decisions, PII findings, model, cost, hashes, and signature for later review.
